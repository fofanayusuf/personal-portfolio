What Does Passion Mean to You?

Passion isn’t motivation. It’s the force that shapes your reality.

January 1, 2026 · yfofana

Getting pissed off with yourself after watching others display their wins on the internet, their lifestyle seems glamorous and flawless, you hate your life against the wall of regrets, fighting to stop the earth and take what you deserve, for you, that's passion?

“Fighting to stop the earth and take what you deserve — is that passion?”

Obsessed endlessly with that one skill that keeps dragging you back on the surface, you don't get to understand why progress is too slow. You keep driving. That's passion for you?

“You don’t understand why progress is slow. You keep driving.”

Passion means staying on the surface when everything above you is pressing you down, fighting for your spot, and refusing to get drown.

Only you have the eyes to see it, the opportunity you can't stop imagining, but again, how much it takes, the space between where you are and how far you need to climb is a glass that seems difficult to swallow.

“It takes passion to swallow the glass.”

What's that one thing that keeps you up late at night, that's your passion?

For most people, it is an unanswered question. The question doesn't have to be philosophical, something basic like “who am I”?

“What keeps you awake at night is trying to tell you something.”

Steve Jobs said, “People with passion can change the world.”

He changed the world only because he was passionate about technology and how it can make a difference in this world.

“People with passion can change the world.” — Steve Jobs

Let's get personal for a second. I always write what I'm going through, in a low hanging fruit way that most people can relate to.

I am obsessed with taking my own fate into my hands, instead of letting circumstances dictate my DESIRE REALITY.

“No one determines my DESIRE REALITY but me.”

For me, I found my passion a few years ago. I am passionate about changing my life through daily actions.

Small actions. Repeated daily. Proof stacked over time.

“A sequence of steps stacks up to dictate your DESIRE REALITY.”

When you have a north star, something that hangs you on a thread, just a little bit of hope, then you have something to live for.

Every wind that blows the average person off course will bounce off your skin.

“When you have something to live for, pressure cannot drown you.”

Have you found something that the world can count on?

If not, don’t settle. Keep searching.

What are you passionate about?

You find it.
