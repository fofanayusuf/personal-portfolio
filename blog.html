The Internet Is The New Rich

Written by Yusuf Admirable Fofana • August 2025

Money no longer comes from job titles. It comes from Wi-Fi, curiosity, and skills shared online.

“Play long-term games with long-term people.” – Naval Ravikant

The new rich are creators, coders, designers, and problem solvers who build and share online.

Curiosity is their fuel. Skills are their tools. The internet is their distribution.

“The internet is the ultimate equalizer. Anyone with curiosity and discipline can become a creator of value.” – Dan Koe

Why This Matters

In a world where attention is the new currency, learning to create and share your work is freedom.

You don’t need a boss to validate you. You need an audience to resonate with your ideas.

“Seek wealth, not money or status. Wealth is assets that earn while you sleep.” – Naval Ravikant

The New Path

  • Learn skills: Design, coding, writing, video, AI, and strategy.
  • Show your work: Blogs, YouTube, social media, or open-source projects.
  • Build digital leverage: Products, courses, tools, apps.
  • Earn freedom: Replace job income with online income, scale with systems.

The focus isn’t on quick hacks or viral moments. It’s about compounding knowledge and consistently creating value.

Patience, curiosity, and leverage will outperform brute force and job security every time.

“Become the best version of yourself. Share generously. Build compounding assets.” – Dan Koe

The internet allows anyone to play the long game. Your ideas, your creations, and your knowledge can reach millions without intermediaries.

True freedom isn’t just wealth—it’s autonomy over your time, attention, and decisions.
